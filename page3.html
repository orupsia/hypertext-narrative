The Academy

Coriolanus Snow attened The Academy where he excelled academically; one of the brightest in his class.

Behind closed doors however, Coriolanus Snow was starving. The Snow family had no money, no food, and lived life like something from the districts.

No, no, they weren't anything like district slum. At the end of the day, the Snows had class. Snows had the future President of Panem, Coriolanus Snow.
